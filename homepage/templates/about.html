Kalimudan is a Non-Government Organization (NGO) based in Marawi City, Lanao Del Sur. In its almost 35 years of existence, Kalimudan has developed into a well-established organization working with lean and mean staffing patterns, a backstopped by external experts - consultants, allies or members of the Board who are from the academe.


Kalimudan History

In the year 1988, Kalimudan was established and set up by young professional volunteers, members from academe, religious groups, and bonded themselves in order to voluntarily share their acquired skills, talents, and expertise to benefit poor and marginalized sectors of the society. In its succeeding years, it evolved into a well-developed, systematic, and institutionalized NGO.

Believing that political stability, inter-cultural harmony, quality education and healthy population are keys to peace and development, it has consistenyl worked in promoting solidarity and harmonious relations between and among citizens. Civil Society Organizations and Local Government working together for peace and development.

Kalimudan's current work covers 4 clusters of activities, including (major programs, special projects, contracted programs, and relief to internally displaced people). Under major programs are peace building, sustainable resource management, education, health, and enhancing local governance.
