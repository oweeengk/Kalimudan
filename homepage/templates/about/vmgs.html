Vision

Kalimudan envisions an empowered community living in peace with sustainable livelihood and a healthy environment.

.

Mission

Kalimudan commits itself to enhance the capacities of its partners in development as they strive to build peace while securing their livelihood. Improving health standards and nurturing the environment to uplive the well-being of the people of Lanao.

.

Goals

Kalimudan will be known as a center for dialogue and Maranao organizing. Be a center bridge between the academe and the communities as a source of technology, have developed and discussed a maranao consciousness and control of its own organizational development and management system.
