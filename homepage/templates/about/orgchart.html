Organizational Chart



READ: KRFI Organizational Chart

The organizational chart of Kalimudan sa Ranao Foundation Inc. is depicted in the above image, illustrating a well-structured hierarchy to efficiently manage the organization's diverse functions and programs. At the apex of the structure is the Board of Directors, serving as the governing body responsible for strategic decision-making and overarching policy guidance.

Directly reporting to the Board of Directors is the Executive Director, who holds a pivotal role in overseeing the day-to-day operations and ensuring the successful implementation of the Foundation's mission and goals. Below the Executive Director, the Chief Operating Officer (COO) takes charge, overseeing critical administrative and financial aspects to maintain the organization's stability and sustainability.

Under the COO, there are three primary branches: Administration and Finance, which handles the financial aspects and general administration of the organization; Monitoring, Evaluation, Accountability, and Learning (MEAL), which focuses on assessing program effectiveness and impact; and Human Resource Management, responsible for the recruitment, development, and management of the organization's human capital.

Further branching under the COO, the Management Information System team works to streamline and enhance the organization's data and information management processes.

Continuing the organizational structure, a second layer under the COO encompasses various programmatic departments, each dedicated to a specific area of focus. These include Participative Governance and CVE (Countering Violent Extremism), Education Literacy Program, Peacebuilding Program, Sustainable Livelihood Program, and Disaster Preparedness and Community-Based Climate Change Adaptation (CCA). Additionally, the TVIS (Technical and Vocational Instruction System) Program is situated within this layer, contributing to the Foundation's educational initiatives.

This well-organized structure reflects a holistic approach to community development, demonstrating the Foundation's commitment to addressing diverse needs across multiple sectors, from education and peacebuilding to sustainable livelihoods and disaster preparedness. The delineation of roles and responsibilities within the organizational chart ensures effective coordination and collaboration to achieve the Foundation's overarching mission in the Ranao region.
